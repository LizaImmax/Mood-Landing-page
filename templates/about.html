About Mood Journal

Inspiration

The project was inspired by my girlfriend who lives with Bipolar 2, which is a mood disorder. Bipolar II disorder is diagnosed when a person has a pattern of depressive and hypomanic episodes but not full-blown manic episodes as in bipolar I disorder. We thought it was a good idea to keep track of her mood one afternoon after a doctor's visit.
