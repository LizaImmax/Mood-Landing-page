What is Mood Tracking?

Mood tracking is the practice of regularly recording and analyzing your emotional state, feelings, and overall well-being. It allows you to keep a digital diary of your moods and provides valuable insights into your emotional health over time.

With our Mood Tracking Web App, you can easily log and visualize your moods, helping you gain a better understanding of your emotions and identify patterns in your feelings.

Benefits of Mood Tracking

Mood tracking offers several benefits:

  • Improved self-awareness
  • Early detection of mood changes
  • Identification of triggers and stressors
  • Better mental health management
  • Enhanced communication with healthcare professionals
